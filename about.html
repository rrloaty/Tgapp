📖 About DiceMint

DiceMint is your fun and rewarding crypto WebApp that allows you to earn money by tapping, betting, and inviting others!

Choose a number between 1–6 and place your bet. If the dice lands on your number, you win double your money!

💸 Tap & Earn

Tap the button as many times as you want — each tap earns you 1 coin, which equals $0.01. The more you tap, the more you earn!

🎁 Claim Bonus

Join our Telegram group and channel to claim a free $10 bonus instantly in your wallet.

🤝 Refer & Earn

Share your referral link and earn $5 for every friend that joins DiceMint using your unique link.

💵 Withdraw

Once your balance reaches at least $250, you can submit a withdrawal request to receive real funds in your BNB wallet.

We do not require login. Your balance is stored safely in your browser and connected to your Telegram account. Never clear your browser or you may lose progress.
